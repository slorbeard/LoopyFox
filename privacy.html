InsulinCalc & Game

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information InsulinCalc collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

**Information We Collect** InsulinCalc collects the personal information you voluntarily enter during setup: your name, year of birth, sex, and email address. It also stores health-related calibration values. All of this information is stored locally on your device only.

**How Your Data is Stored** All data you enter into InsulinCalc is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your personal or health data. Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

**Data we do NOT collect** We do not collect your location, device identifiers, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or any biometric data. We do not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking pixels of any kind.

**Health Data** The blood glucose readings, carbohydrate values, and insulin dose calculations you enter are processed entirely on your device. This health data is never transmitted to InsulinCalc's creator or any third party.

**Children's Privacy** InsulinCalc is not directed at children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian is using this app on behalf of a child, they are responsible for supervising all data entry and dosing decisions.

**Your Rights** Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You may delete your data at any time by clearing the app's local storage or uninstalling the app.

**Changes to This Policy** We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date shown at the top of this page.
